Frequently Asked Questions

Unlocking Your Investment Potential

  • How Can I Invest with Tagus Investment Fund?
    To invest with Tagus, you must meet the requirements of an Expert Investor. You can begin by contacting us for more details on subscription processes and investment opportunities.
  • What Are the Minimum Investment Requirements?
    The minimum investment required for each investor is USD 100,000, unless you meet the criteria of a "Sophisticated Investor" as defined under the Mauritius Securities Act 2005.
  • What Is the Risk Associated with Investments in the Fund?
    Investment in the Fund carries inherent risks, including market volatility, liquidity risks, and currency risks. We recommend that potential investors carefully review the risks outlined in the Fund's offering documents before proceeding.
