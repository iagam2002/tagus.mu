FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I invest with Tagus Investment Fund PCC?
    To invest with us, you must be an accredited or expert investor. Contact us for more details on the subscription process and investor requirements.
  • What is the minimum investment required?
    The minimum investment for most of our funds is USD 100,000, although this may vary based on the type of investment product you choose.
  • What are the risks associated with investing in the fund?
    As with any investment, there are inherent risks such as market volatility, currency risk, and liquidity risk. We provide detailed risk assessments and guidance to all investors.
  • What is your approach to portfolio diversification?
    We focus on diversifying investments across multiple asset classes and geographies to mitigate risks and maximize returns. Our strategies are designed to match the investment goals of each investor.
